  • How I became a fan of Montessori educational method?

    Education has been close to my heart already since childhood. I have always enjoyed learning new things and sharing sharing my knowledge with others. Nevetheless the educational system I grew up with was very traditional and teacher-centred. My teacher's training was an life-changing eye-opener on all the existing methods. But it was only after becoming a mother myself that I exploring the alternative educational methods and became trully conviced and inspired discovered Montessori. I was fascinated by the child-centred approach, the emphasis on independence, freedom within limits, and respect for a child's natural psychological development. Since then I've been continously deepening my knowledge of the Montessori method, applying these principles in my own parenting journey.
